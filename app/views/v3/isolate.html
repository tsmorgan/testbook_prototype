Self-isolate by not leaving home

Based on your answers, you should be self-isolating. Self-isolation begins on the day symptoms start and continues for the next 10 full days.

Self-isolation is when you don't leave your home because you have or might have coronavirus. This helps stop the virus speading to other people.

You can leave your home to get a test.

How to self-isolate
  • Don't go to work, school or any public places - work from home if you can
  • Don't go out to get food and medicine – order it online or by phone, or ask someone to bring it to your home
  • Don't have visitors in your home, including friends and family – except for people providing essential care
  • Don't go on public transport or use taxis
  • Don't go out to exercise – exercise at home or in your garden, if you have one

There's support available to help you self-isolate.
